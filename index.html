✂️ Tab Scissors - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Overview

Tab Scissors is a browser extension designed to help users manage and organize their browser tabs efficiently. This privacy policy explains how Tab Scissors handles user information.

Tab Scissors does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Tab Scissors operates with a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • We do not access your browsing history
  • We do not track your web activity
  • We do not store information about websites you visit
  • We do not collect any analytics or usage statistics
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

How Tab Scissors Works

Tab Scissors operates entirely within your local browser environment:

  • All tab management actions happen locally on your device
  • The extension only accesses tab information necessary for its core functionality (moving, closing, organizing tabs)
  • No data is sent to external servers or third parties
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No internet connection required for functionality

Permissions Used

Tab Scissors requests minimal permissions for its core functionality:

  • Tabs permission: To read tab titles and URLs for organization purposes
  • Storage permission: To temporarily store undo information locally in your browser
  • ActiveTab permission: To interact with your current tab for position-relative operations

All permissions are used solely for the extension's core tab management functionality.

Local Data Storage

The only data stored by Tab Scissors is:

  • Temporary undo information (tab URLs and titles) stored locally in your browser storage
  • This information is automatically deleted after 10 minutes or when you close your browser
  • No data is stored on external servers or cloud services
  • All storage is handled by your browser's built-in storage APIs

Third-Party Services

Tab Scissors does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs.

Data Security

Since Tab Scissors doesn't collect or transmit data:

  • There are no data breaches possible
  • No personal information can be compromised
  • All operations are contained within your local browser environment

Children's Privacy

Tab Scissors does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of Tab Scissors after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source

Tab Scissors is designed with transparency in mind. Users can inspect the extension's code to verify these privacy practices.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Tab Scissors, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

This extension respects your privacy and operates with minimal data access solely for its intended tab management functionality.
